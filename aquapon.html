All

This mode allows for all water quality parameters to be tested at once. Use the inputs below to check the suitability of each parameter for the chosen fish. The colour of the input will change to red if it is too high and to blue if it is to low. Green indicates the level is suitable.

Press the "Start simulation" button to simulate a live recording of each parameter. Values will randomly increase or decrease every second and each parameter can be observed for changes. Parameters that are not suitable will have higher probabilities of adjusting to the fit conditions, simulating what an automatic water parameter adjusters. For example, if the water is too hot, the simulation will have a higher probability to reduce the heat caused by a water cooler being activated in the system.
